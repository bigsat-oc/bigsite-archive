    What the B.I.G. Retreat Is All About

    The Bioinformatics Graduate Program (BIOF), Interdisciplinary Oncology Program (IOP), and Genome Science + Technology Graduate Program (GSAT) from the University of British Columbia are pleased to present their 6th annual research day on March 11th, 2016.

     

    In this event, over 100 graduate students from all three programs would gather to present their exciting research projects and academic/industry professionals are invited to talk about their research/expertise in seminars and workshops.

     

     

    Would you like to present your research?
    Are you curious about our students' research projects?
    Would you like to hear about novel technologies from professionals?
